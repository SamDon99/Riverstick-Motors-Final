Our Testing Services

Comprehensive vehicle testing services to keep you safe on the road

CVR Testing

Certified CVR testing for Light (LCV) and Heavy (HCV) Commercial Vehicles, ensuring RSA compliance and fast turnaround times.

NSAI Approved ATC Centre

Official NSAI-certified Approved Test Centre (ATC) providing certified vehicle testing and compliance services to national standards.

Declaration of Conversion of Vehicles

Complete assistance with vehicle conversions, documentation, and inspection to meet RSA and legal compliance.

SQI & Modification Reports

SQI-certified inspections, safety reports, and official modification certifications for converted or altered vehicles.

Tachograph Calibration

Expert calibration and repairs of digital and analogue tachographs for commercial vehicles.

Road Speed Limiter Calibration & Repairs

Specialised calibration and repairs of road speed limiters to meet safety regulations and enhance vehicle compliance.

About Riverstick Motors CVR Testing Centre

Founded in 1968 as a family-run garage, Riverstick Motors has over 57 years of experience in the Irish automotive industry. Originally established as a general vehicle service and repair centre, we expanded into CVR (Commercial Vehicle Roadworthiness) testing in 1982. Since then, we've grown to become one of Ireland's most trusted CVR Testing Centres.

With over 43 years of specialised CVR testing experience, we have tested more than 500,000 vehicles – continually delivering certified, reliable, and efficient service to our valued customers.

In 2024 alone, over 7,043 vehicles were tested at our centre – a testament to our reputation for professionalism, efficiency, and dedication to road safety.
